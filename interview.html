Module 10

Interview Questions & Troubleshooting

Prepare for beginner to advanced DevOps interviews with practical Jenkins scenarios.

Beginner Questions

  • What is Jenkins and why is it used?
  • Difference between CI and CD?
  • What is a Jenkinsfile?

Intermediate Questions

  • Declarative vs Scripted pipelines?
  • How do webhooks trigger Jenkins?
  • How do you handle credentials securely?

Advanced Scenario Questions

  • How to reduce queue time in large organizations?
  • How would you design HA Jenkins?
  • How do you standardize pipelines at scale?

Troubleshooting Cases

  • Pipeline stuck in queue: verify node labels/executors and offline agents.
  • Git checkout failed: rotate token, check SSH keys and known hosts.
  • OutOfMemoryError: increase JVM heap and reduce plugin overhead.

Mock Interview Task

Design a pipeline for microservice deployment on Kubernetes with rollback strategy and approval gate for production.
